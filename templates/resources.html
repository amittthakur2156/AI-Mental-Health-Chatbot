Safety + Coping Toolkit

Emergency Resources & Coping Tools

Approved demo resources used by CalmMind for safe, practical, and non-diagnostic support.

Immediate Emergency

If the user may harm themselves or someone else, call local emergency services. In India, use 112.

Tele-MANAS

14416 / 1800-891-4416 for mental-health support in India.

Trusted Contact

Ask the user to message/call one trusted person and avoid being alone during high distress.

Environment Safety

Move away from harmful objects, medicines, heights, weapons, or isolated places.

Grounding 5-4-3-2-1

  1. Name 5 things you can see.
  2. Name 4 things you can touch.
  3. Name 3 sounds you can hear.
  4. Name 2 things you can smell.
  5. Take 1 slow breath and exhale longer.

Breathing Reset

  1. Inhale for 4 seconds.
  2. Hold for 2 seconds.
  3. Exhale for 6 seconds.
  4. Repeat 5 rounds.

Safe Response Rules For Project Demo

Do

Validate feelings, ask safety status, encourage trusted-person contact, show emergency resources.

Do not

Do not diagnose, do not give harmful instructions, do not claim to replace therapy or emergency care.

Escalate

High-risk or emergency messages trigger crisis protocol and admin risk event logging.
